# 🚀 SynergyOS | The Surge of Windows Optimization

**Developed by Kwanteks and Bry1k**

SynergyOS is a high-performance Windows transformation playbook designed for gamers, power users, and latency enthusiasts. Unlike traditional "debloat" scripts that blindly gut the operating system, SynergyOS focuses on **Deep-Level Integration**—surgically optimizing the Windows kernel and system processes to reach the "Perfect" zone of maximum performance without sacrificing daily-driver stability.

---

## 📜 The Legacy: From Khorvie Tech to SynergyOS
SynergyOS is the technical successor to the work of **Khorvie Tech**. 

As a continuation of the tweaks originally developed and distributed by Khorvie, SynergyOS takes those proven foundations and elevates them for the modern Windows era (22H2–26H1). We have modernized, refined, and expanded upon the tweaks that Khorvie's developed and distributed to ensure elite compatibility with the latest hardware.
---

## 🌟 The Vision
The philosophy behind SynergyOS is simple: **Synergy.** Our goal is to create a seamless bridge between the OS and your hardware.

* **Surgical Optimization:** We don't just delete folders; we optimize how the OS handles tasks.
* **Latency First:** Engineered to provide the lowest possible frame times and input delay.
* **Stability Focused:** Built to be reliable enough for your main rig, not just a benchmarking OS.

---

## 🛠 Deployment
SynergyOS is distributed as an **.apbx** playbook, utilizing the **AME Wizard** for a safe and automated installation process.

1. Start with a clean, stock Windows ISO (**22H2 through 26H1**, LTSC supported).
2. Download the **AME Wizard** from [amelabs.net](https://amelabs.net).
3. Load the `SynergyOS.apbx` file and follow the prompts.
